# AI-Based-Student-Career-Education-Advisor
AI-Based Student Career &amp; Education Advisor is a web application that helps students choose suitable careers and courses based on their interests, skills, and academic performance. It uses AI and Machine Learning to provide personalized career recommendations and learning suggestions, helping students make better educational and career decisions.

Problem Statement

Many students face challenges in selecting the right career and educational path due to a lack of proper guidance, limited awareness of career opportunities, and uncertainty about their interests and abilities. Traditional career counseling is often not personalized and may not consider a student's academic performance, skills, and career goals. As a result, students may choose unsuitable courses or careers, leading to dissatisfaction and reduced career growth. Therefore, there is a need for an AI-based system that provides accurate, personalized, and data-driven career guidance to help students make informed educational and career decisions.

Problem Objective

The main objective of this project is to develop an AI-Based Student Career & Education Advisor that assists students in choosing the most suitable career and educational path based on their interests, skills, academic performance, and career goals. The system uses Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to analyze student data and provide personalized career recommendations, suggest relevant courses and certifications, identify skill gaps, and recommend learning resources. The project aims to simplify career planning, improve decision-making, and help students prepare for successful future careers.
